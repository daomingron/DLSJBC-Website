Admissions

Join the DLSJBC Community

De La Salle John Bosco College welcomes students from nursery to college and provides a clear path for enrollment through a guided admissions process.

Enrollment Steps

  • See the guidance-in-charge for the entrance examination or interview.
  • Secure the eligibility form from the principal's office.
  • Present the needed documents and registration form to the registrar's office.
  • Proceed to the finance office for tuition assessment and pay the enrollment fee.

Common Requirements

  • PSA birth certificate and school records or certificate of completion.
  • Certificate of good moral character.
  • Medical certificate and recent ID picture.
  • Additional documents for transfer, college, or TVET applicants.
